Financial Assistance

Jumpstart offers funding to assist with registration fees; follow the steps below:

  • Register with Un Sueño Soccer & Fitness Academy (Un Sueño Registration); don’t make the payment.
  • Contact the academy at soccer@ussfa.ca to indicate that you will apply for Jumpstart funding.
  • Submit your Jumpstart application:
Apply for Jumpstart Funding
  • Jumpstart will notify the applicant and Un Sueño Soccer & Fitness Academy of the results of your application.
  • If funding has not been granted, please contact the academy at soccer@ussfa.ca to discuss arrangements for the unpaid balance.
